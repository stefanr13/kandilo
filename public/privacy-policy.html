Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Kandilo is a parish communication and community platform for Orthodox Christian churches. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how to contact us about privacy questions.

Information We Collect

  • Account information such as your name, email address, and authentication provider.
  • Profile information you choose to add, such as phone number, ministry involvement, and biography.
  • Church membership data, invitations, posts, events, and bulletin activity related to your parish access.
  • Donation records needed to process payments and issue receipts.
  • Push notification tokens used to deliver parish updates to your devices.

How We Use Information

  • To authenticate users and manage access to parish-specific content.
  • To operate church communication features such as posts, events, invitations, and notifications.
  • To process donations and deliver donation receipts.
  • To improve reliability, prevent abuse, and support customer requests.

Payments

Donations are processed through Stripe. Kandilo does not store full payment card details on its own servers.

Third-Party Services

Kandilo uses Firebase for authentication, database, storage, hosting, and messaging; Resend for transactional email; and Stripe for payments.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data is shared only with service providers needed to run the app or when required by law.

Retention

We retain data for as long as needed to operate parish services, meet legal obligations, and maintain financial records. You can contact us to request account deletion, subject to records we must retain.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact privacy@kandilo.org.
